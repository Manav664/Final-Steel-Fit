About us

Our organisation, "STEELFIT ENGINEERING CO," was founded in Ahmedabad in 1983. Known for producing and supplying a variety of Dished ends, Pipe Caps, Concentric & Eccentric Tori-cone, Fabricated Expansion Bellows, Coil Bending, and Swage Connection in all types of C.S. and S.S., Alloy steel, Aluminum Material of Construction in accordance with Clients drawing, Specification, and Code, our company is a reputable organisation.

We are an ISO:9001,2000 Certified Company (TUV Inspection)

We are also IBR (Indian Boiler Regulations) Approved

Since many years ago, we have collaborated with LRA, Bureau Veritas, TUV, EIL, PDIL, Jacob H & G, DNV, SGS, UHDE INDIA LTD, TATA PROJECTS LTD, BARC, and several other reputable consultants and inspection agencies. We have made a reputation for ourselves in the industry in just a few short years. With creative concepts, top-notch products, and a commitment to providing you with better service, "STEELFIT ENGINEERING CO." has established itself as a reputable brand both domestically and abroad.

We supply the needs of the Indian and international markets for the power industry, petrochemicals, fertilisers, water treatment facilities, cement manufacturing, agricultural machinery, space manufacturing, etc. We have partnerships with several TPIs, including LRA, TUV, TUV sud, BVIPL, SGS, DNV GL, IBR, JACOB, and TATA.
